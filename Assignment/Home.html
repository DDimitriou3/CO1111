What is Treasure Hunt App?

Treasure Hunt App is an online quiz where the answers depend on your finding information at specific locations using the answers from the quiz and the QR codes.Players must be at specific locations to use the QR codes to help them contain the questions. Every correct answer closer to the Treasure. After competing the questions and the other findings during the hunt all the information must be collected to find the Treasure and the end of the game. Treasure Hunt is an online quiz game which the organisers prepare a specific list of missions or quizzes, which the participants seek to gather or complete all the quizzes and find the right QR code to "unlock" more quizzes to get ahead of their goal .Usually the participants work in small groups ,maximum of 5 people ,but the rules allow individuals to participate but this makes them more difficult to win.The goal is to complete the list or to complete the most of the quizzes.In variations of the game ,players scan QR codes to find new challenges,answer multiple choice questions ,numerical questions,boolean questions and some textual questions with the theme topic of the hunt.
